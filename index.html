Shelby Soutter

Software Developer

My journey into software engineering has not been a traditional one. I started my undergraduate career at UNC Chapel Hill as a pre-PA student, hoping to one day become a Pediatric Oncologist PA. After graduating with a Classics degree and minors in Biology and Philosophy in 2018, I then went on to apply and get accepted to PA school. Shortly after, one of my friends introduced me to the idea of going into software engineering. Up until then, I had had no desire to go into the tech field, nor did I know that I could love the industry as much as I have come to. I started learning the basics of Python and fell in love with it, which then prompted me to apply to the Momentum program. From there, the rest is history…
